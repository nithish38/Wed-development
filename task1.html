Toji Fushiguro

Toji Fushiguro (伏黒 甚爾, Fushiguro Tōji), originally Toji Zenin, is a former member of the powerful Zenin clan and the father of Megumi Fushiguro. He’s one of the most feared figures in the Jujutsu Kaisen universe because of his immense physical abilities and his complete lack of cursed energy — earning him the nickname “The Sorcerer Killer.”
